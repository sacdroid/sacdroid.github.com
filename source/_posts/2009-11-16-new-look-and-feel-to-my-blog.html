Y'day night,I changed the whole look and feel of this blog. Isn't it cool?

I managed to add header image following this video and header image is captured somewhere near to GOA by one my team mate during our last trip.

and background colour is changed by applying my html skills. I picked colour from header image using gimp and edited blog html (Dashboard->Layout->Edit HTML) to add this background colour to body, sidebar and header itself.
